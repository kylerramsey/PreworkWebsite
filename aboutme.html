About Me



Different Seasons of Life



For most of my life, I have been on the move. This comes with a lot of difficulties, like having to make new friends, find new jobs, and change schools. It's not the type of upbringing that I would recommend to everyone neccesarily, but for me, I truly feel it was a blessing. Because I've lived in so many different places, I've been able to experience so many different things. With each new place comes a different culture and climate, and brings on their own uniqueness.

Above are a few of the places I have lived for a significant amount of time. There's so many sides you can see to every place on earth, but I tried my best with these pictures to give a sense of the feeling and vibe of each location. There are things that I love and don't love as much about every place I've lived, but I have to say, that after having lived in Portland, I truly do not think the cold is for me. I applaud all that can live through even worse winters, because I need my sunlight! There's a lot more I could say about where I come from, or the kind of person I am, but I really look forward to getting to know you all personally, and hearing about your experiences as well!
