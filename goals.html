Goals During Bootcamp



The thing that will make (or break, god forbid) me in almost any big change in life, is staying locked in and focused. I really have no issue learning new things, and have absolutely no problem being the least knowledgable person in a room, in fact, I prefer it that way. I would say my main issue, is once having learned and practiced a task, sticking to it, and not just jumping straight into the next thing.


Thankfully, I have some many lines of support to push me and keep me focused and on the right path. I have the wonderful folks at Coding Temple, who I'm already starting to know and love, as well as the support of my amazing girlfriend, who is also enrolled in our cohort. I will say, it sometimes feels like we are cheating, because having someone always there to hold you accountable, and get you up out of bed, whether or not you want to on a given day, feels like a superpower. Not everyone has that luxury and I'm super grateful for it.


To the right, you can find a playlist I've created as one line of defense against my lack of focus. Music that keeps me engaged, but doesn't distract me is massively important for keeping me locked in. Feel free to save the playlist, or grab a few songs from it!
